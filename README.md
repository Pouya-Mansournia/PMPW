## About Pouya Mansournia

Pouya Mansournia is a robotics and mechatronics engineer, systems architect, technical founder, and product leader with more than a decade of experience developing intelligent machines and industrial systems.

His work spans the full engineering lifecycle—from early-stage research, system architecture, and mechanical design to embedded control, prototyping, validation, production, and real-world deployment.

His main areas of expertise include:

- Autonomous mobile robots and robotic platforms
- Warehouse and material-handling automation
- Embedded electronics and control systems
- Precision motion and nano-positioning systems
- Mechatronic product development
- Robotics system architecture
- Technical leadership and engineering strategy
